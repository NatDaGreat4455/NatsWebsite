The Rules to the Undertale and Deltarune Impossible Nuzlocke

Suggested by NatDaGreat4455

  1. You must beat ALL routes of that game (Undertale: Neutral, Pacifist, Genocide)(Deltarune. True Pacifist, Full Massacre, Snowgrave)
  2. You must fight ALL ENEMY'S (including secret bosses and all encounters like gyftroot, mouse wheel ect.)
  3. If you die once, you FULLY reset the run (meaning if I finished all of pacifist and died on massacre, I would have to delete BOTH saves and restart from the beginning)(this includes both games)
  4. You can't use any healing items IN BATTLE but you can use healing items outside of battle
  5. NO EQUIPTABLES (besides the rings while doing snowgrave)
  6. Ice Shock, Snowgrave, and Pirouette are the only things you can use tp on in battle
  7. Complete BOTH games with rules 1-5
