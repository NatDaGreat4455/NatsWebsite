The Rules to the Undertale and Deltarune Nuzlocke

Suggested by NatDaGreat4455

  1. You must beat ALL routes of that game (meaning after a neutral run, you have to reset and then start pacifist or genocide) (Undertale: Neutral, Pacifist, Genocide) (Deltarune: True Pacifist, Full Massacre, Snowgrave)
  2. You must fight ALL ENEMY'S (including secret bosses and all encounters like gyftrot, mouse wheel etc.)
  3. If you die once, you FULLY reset the run (meaning if I finished all of pacifist and died on massacre, I would have to delete BOTH saves and restart from the beginning)
